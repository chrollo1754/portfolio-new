What I Do

Services That
Move the Needle

I keep things simple — you tell me what your business needs, I build something that actually works. No jargon, no unnecessary upsells, just results.

01
Custom Website Development

Built from scratch, made to fit your brand. I don't use off-the-shelf templates — everything is written specifically for you so it actually looks like you, not like everyone else.

02
Landing Page Design

One page, one goal — get people to take action. Whether that's booking a call, filling a form, or buying something, I build landing pages that are made to convert.

03
Business Websites

Your website is often the first impression a customer gets of your business. I make sure that impression is a good one — clean, professional, and easy to use.

04
Portfolio Websites

If your work is good, your website should show it off properly. I build portfolio sites that let your work do the talking and get potential clients reaching out.

05
Speed Optimization

A slow website loses customers before they even see what you offer. I clean up the code, optimize images, and make sure your site loads fast on every device.

06
Mobile Responsive Design

Most people will visit your site from their phone. Everything I build looks and works just as well on a 6-inch screen as it does on a desktop — no pinching or zooming required.

About Me

Just a Guy Who
Loves Building Stuff

Developer Yashraj Chavan

Hey — I'm Yashraj. I got into web development because I genuinely enjoy building things that work. Not just things that look good in a screenshot, but websites that actually load fast, feel smooth, and get the person visiting them to do something.

I've worked on jewellery stores, fitness brands, restaurants, and creative portfolios. Every project teaches me something new. I don't have a massive agency behind me — it's just me, which means you get direct communication, proper attention to your project, and someone who actually cares how it turns out.

I'm based in India but I work with clients everywhere. If you've got an idea — big or small — let's figure it out together.

I Keep it Simple

No fluff, no unnecessary complexity. Just clean work that does what it's supposed to.

I'm Easy to Reach

You won't be stuck waiting 3 days for a reply. I'm responsive and I keep you in the loop.

I Care About Speed

A slow website is a broken website. Every site I build is optimised to load fast.

Results First

A pretty website that doesn't bring in business is just decoration. I build with goals in mind.
